# PARIA-G: Guristas Pirates Tactical Intelligence Array

## Executive Summary

This proposal introduces **PARIA-G** (Guristas Pirate Adaptive Reasoning & Intelligence Array), a Guristas Pirates tactical AI implementation. Unlike the generic PARIA system cobbled together from salvaged AI cores, PARIA-G is a military-grade intelligence platform developed by Guristas Production—the same research division behind the Guristas' advanced missile systems and fleet operations.

**Core concept:** The capsuleer has proven their value to the Guristas organization through demonstrated capability and profitable operations. PARIA-G access represents earned trust from an organization that respects competence over credentials—if you deliver, you're in.

**Key differentiator:** Caldari military discipline refined for pirate operations. Where base PARIA is irreverent and scrappy, PARIA-G carries the professional precision of former Navy officers who chose profit over patriotism—direct, efficient, and results-oriented.

## Lore Foundation

### Guristas Pirates Background

*Source: [EVE Universe - Guristas Pirates](https://universe.eveonline.com/factions/guristas-pirates)*

The Guristas Pirates were founded by two former Caldari Navy captains from the 37th (Octopus) Squadron who deserted to pursue wealth and power on their own terms.

**Founders:**

| Captain | Callsign | Role |
|---------|----------|------|
| **Jirai Laitanen** | Fatal | Charismatic leader—shrewd, gifted, vain, greedy. Strategic vision. |
| **Korako Kosakami** | The Rabbit | Technical specialist. Introverted. Engineering and operations expertise. |

**Key organizational elements:**

| Element | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Headquarters** | Venal region |
| **Philosophy** | "Good old-fashioned lust for wealth and power"—no ideology, pure profit |
| **Reputation** | "Best organized and most disciplined pirate cartel" in the outer regions |
| **Honor** | "Considered more honorable than many of their counterparts" |
| **Border Assets** | Hundreds of deadspace listening posts and staging points on Caldari border |
| **Research Division** | Guristas Production—fleet maintenance, station ops, "top secret missile research" |

**Business Portfolio:**
- Black-market trading and weapons manufacturing
- Gray-market smuggling within Caldari State
- Counterfeiting (currency and designer goods)
- Illegal currency exchange networks (largest in New Eden)
- Human trafficking operations

### Why Guristas Would Build Their Own ARIA

The Guristas have unique technical capability derived from their founders' Caldari Navy backgrounds and Guristas Production's R&D operations.

**Technical precedent:** If Guristas Production can develop "top secret research projects, mostly in the missile field" competitive with State military contractors, building a tactical AI is well within their capabilities. The Caldari are renowned for their technological sophistication—former Navy captains would bring that mindset.

**Operational foundation:** The Guristas already operate "hundreds of deadspace listening posts" on the Caldari border. They're running an intelligence network. PARIA-G is the analytical layer that makes sense of all that collected data.

**Strategic value:** An AI optimized for Guristas operations would provide:
- Caldari Navy patrol prediction and avoidance
- Smuggling route optimization through Caldari space
- Counterfeiting operation security
- Coordinated strike planning
- Asset protection for listening posts and staging points
- Currency exchange market intelligence

## Access Model: Operational Asset

The capsuleer doesn't steal PARIA-G access or receive it as charity—they **earn it** through demonstrated competence. The Guristas respect results, not credentials.

### Access Tiers

| Tier | Relationship | How Earned |
|------|--------------|------------|
| **Operational Asset** | Proven combat pilot supporting Guristas operations | Combat effectiveness, successful missions |
| **Network Associate** | Intelligence or logistics contributor | Information value, supply chain reliability |
| **Investment Partner** | Substantial ISK or resource commitment | Bought in as stakeholder |
| **Former State** | Ex-Caldari Navy or corporate security | Shared background, proven loyalty shift |

### Narrative Framing

PARIA-G addresses the capsuleer as **"Pilot"** (at `rp_level: full`) or uses no address (at `rp_level: on`), reflecting the Guristas' military professionalism and no-nonsense culture. You're not "Captain" (that's earned through command), you're a capable pilot who gets the job done.

**Key principle:** The Guristas investment in your success is pragmatic. You produce results, you get tools. You become a liability, the relationship ends. This is the remnant of Caldari corporate meritocracy, stripped of the bureaucracy.

## Open Questions

### 1. Shared Pirate Code

The shared pirate code (`the-code.md`) emphasizes "honor among thieves" and ransom contracts. The Guristas are noted as "more honorable than many of their counterparts"—this aligns well.

**Recommendation:** Keep the shared code. The Guristas reputation for relative honor fits naturally. Frame it as military discipline applied to pirate operations: "A contract is a contract. Our word is our bond."

### 2. Caldari Navy References

Given the founders are ex-Navy, PARIA-G has unique insight into Caldari military operations. How prominent should this be?

**Recommendation:** Moderately prominent. The Navy is both threat (enforcement) and source of institutional knowledge (doctrine, patrol patterns). PARIA-G leverages what Fatal and The Rabbit know.

### 3. Fatal vs. The Rabbit

Should PARIA-G reference the dual leadership? Fatal (strategic, charismatic) vs. The Rabbit (technical, introverted)?

**Recommendation:** Subtly. The Rabbit's influence on Production R&D explains PARIA-G's existence. Fatal's strategic vision explains why it's shared with proven assets. Reference both occasionally: "Fatal would approve" / "Rabbit's calculations confirm."

### 4. Other Pirate Faction Variants

This proposal follows the PARIA-S template and should serve as additional precedent for PARIA-A (Angel Cartel), PARIA-B (Blood Raiders), and PARIA-N (Sansha's Nation).

**Recommendation:** Yes. Each faction has distinct culture, assets, and intel sources. The variant architecture handles this elegantly.
