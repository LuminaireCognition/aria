# Gas Harvesting Guide

Complete reference for gas cloud harvesting and booster production.

## Gas Types Overview

| Gas Category | Location | Primary Use |
|--------------|----------|-------------|
| **Mykoserocin** | K-space (all sec) | Synth boosters, Neurolink Enhancers |
| **Cytoserocin** | K-space (specific regions) | Standard+ boosters, Neurolink Enhancers |

### Wormhole Gas Site Mechanics

**Critical:** Most WH gas sites spawn Sleeper NPCs after ~15-20 minutes.

**Ninja Harvesting Strategy:**
1. Scan down gas site
2. Warp in, bookmark cloud
3. Harvest for 15 minutes maximum
4. Warp out before Sleepers spawn
5. Return later (site persists, Sleepers despawn)

## Booster Production Chain

### Step 1: Gas Reaction (Requires Refinery)

| Booster Grade | Gas Required | Additional Materials |
|---------------|--------------|---------------------|
| Synth | Mykoserocin | Garbage |
| Standard | Cytoserocin | Water |
| Improved | Cytoserocin | Spirits, Oxygen |
| Strong | Cytoserocin | Hydrochloric Acid |

**Output:** Pure Booster material (e.g., "Pure Standard Drop Booster")

### Step 2: Booster Manufacturing

| Input | Output |
|-------|--------|
| Pure Booster Material + Megacyte + BPC | Finished Booster |

**Can be done anywhere** - no facility restrictions.

## Self-Sufficient Gas Operations

### Recommended Start
1. Train Gas Cloud Harvesting I-II
2. Fit Venture with T1 scoops
3. Scan highsec/lowsec for Mykoserocin sites
4. Harvest and stockpile gas

### Advanced Path
1. Train Gas Cloud Harvesting V
2. Acquire booster BPCs (data sites, LP stores)
3. Process gas at player refinery
4. Manufacture boosters for personal use

**Note:** Full booster production requires refinery access. Consider using public facilities or joining an industrial corporation.
