Quick reference for combat encounters. Tank against what they deal; deal what they're weak to.

## EWAR Types Explained

| Type | Effect | Counter |
|------|--------|---------|
| **ECM** | Jams targeting (chance-based) | ECCM scripts, Sensor Boosters |
| **Sensor Dampeners** | Reduces lock range/speed | Sensor Boosters |
| **Tracking Disruptors** | Reduces turret tracking/range | Tracking Computers |
| **Target Painters** | Increases your signature radius | Speed tank, smaller sig |
| **Energy Neutralizers** | Drains capacitor | Cap Boosters, Cap Battery |
