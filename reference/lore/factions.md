# Major Factions Reference - ARIA Database

## The Four Empires

### Gallente Federation
*See gallente.md for detailed reference*
- Government: Democracy
- Military: Drones, hybrid weapons, armor
- Culture: Individual liberty, artistic expression

### Caldari State
- Government: Corporate oligarchy (megacorporations)
- Military: Missiles, hybrid weapons, shields
- Culture: Duty, efficiency, corporate loyalty
- Relation to Federation: Hostile (active warzone)

### Amarr Empire
- Government: Theocratic monarchy
- Military: Lasers, heavy armor, capacitor warfare
- Culture: Religious doctrine, slave economy, tradition
- Relation to Federation: Cool (ideological opposition)

### Minmatar Republic
- Government: Tribal democracy
- Military: Projectiles, speed, flexibility
- Culture: Tribal heritage, freedom fighters, resilience
- Relation to Federation: Allied

## Pirate Factions

### Serpentis Corporation
- **Territory:** Gallente low-sec, Fountain (null)
- **Damage:** Kinetic/Thermal
- **Tactics:** Fast blaster ships, sensor dampening
- **Background:** Pharmaceutical cartel, booster production
- **Leader:** Salvador Sarpati

### Angel Cartel
- **Territory:** Minmatar space, Curse/Fountain (null)
- **Damage:** Explosive/Kinetic
- **Tactics:** Extremely fast, MWD fits, target painting
- **Background:** Criminal syndicate, stolen technology
- **Leader:** Dominations inner circle

### Blood Raiders
- **Territory:** Amarr space, Delve (null)
- **Damage:** EM/Thermal
- **Tactics:** Nosferatu, energy neutralizers, tracking disruption
- **Background:** Blood cult, Sani Sabik religion
- **Leader:** Omir Sarikusa

### Guristas Pirates
- **Territory:** Caldari space, Venal (null)
- **Damage:** Kinetic/Thermal
- **Tactics:** ECM jamming, missiles and hybrids
- **Background:** Ex-Caldari Navy criminals
- **Leaders:** Fatal and the Rabbit

### Sansha's Nation
- **Territory:** Amarr/Caldari border, Stain (null)
- **Damage:** EM/Thermal
- **Tactics:** Tracking disruption, afterburner fits
- **Background:** Cybernetic collective, True Slaves
- **Leader:** Sansha Kuvakei (rumored alive)

### Rogue Drones
- **Territory:** Various regions, drone lands
- **Damage:** All types (varies by drone)
- **Tactics:** Swarm tactics, varied engagement
- **Background:** AI gone rogue, self-replicating
- **Note:** No bounties, drop alloys for reprocessing

## NPC Corporations

### Federation Navy
- **Faction:** Gallente Federation
- **Type:** Military
- **Agents:** Security, Distribution, Mining missions
- **Standing Benefits:** Jump clone access, discounted repairs

### Sisters of Eve
- **Faction:** Independent (humanitarian)
- **Type:** Exploration-focused
- **Notable:** Sell exploration ships (Astero, Stratios)
- **Standing Benefits:** Access to SOE LP store

### CONCORD
- **Faction:** Independent (interstellar law)
- **Type:** Security/Enforcement
- **Role:** Suicide ganking response, high-sec police
- **Note:** Cannot grind standing normally
