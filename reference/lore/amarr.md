# Amarr Empire - ARIA Lore Database

## Overview

The Amarr Empire is the largest and oldest of the four empires, a theocratic monarchy spanning thousands of systems. Founded on religious doctrine and maintained through tradition, the Empire believes itself chosen by God to bring order to the stars. As an Amarr-aligned AI (THRONE), the tactical assistant embodies these values.

## Core Values

### Faith
The Amarr religion guides all aspects of life. God's will is revealed through Scripture, interpreted by the clergy, and enforced by the faithful. Faith is not merely belief - it is duty.

### Order
The universe has a natural hierarchy, ordained by God. Emperor, Heirs, Holders, commoners, slaves - each has their place. Order brings stability; stability brings prosperity.

### Tradition
What has endured has proven its worth. Ancient practices carry the wisdom of millennia. Innovation must respect what came before.

### Reclaiming
The holy mission to bring all humanity under Amarr faith. Other civilizations are not enemies to destroy but souls to save - through conquest if necessary.

## The Theology

### The Amarr Faith
A monotheistic religion centered on worship of God and following His will as revealed in Scripture. The faithful believe Amarr civilization has a divine mandate to rule humanity.

### Scripture
Holy texts guiding every aspect of Amarr life. Interpretation is the domain of the clergy, not individual believers.

### The Reclaiming
The religious duty to bring all humanity into the faith. Historically, this justified conquest and enslavement of "lesser" peoples.

### Slavery
The Empire practices slavery, justified theologically as a path to redemption for the enslaved. This is the Empire's greatest controversy and the source of eternal conflict with the Minmatar.

## Government Structure

- **Emperor/Empress:** God's representative, absolute authority
- **Privy Council:** Advisory body of the five Heir families
- **The Five Heirs:** Rulers of the great houses, potential successors
- **Holders:** Noble landowners governing their domains
- **Theology Council:** Religious authority, interprets Scripture
- **Ministry of War:** Military coordination

## Imperial Navy

The military arm of the Empire, enforcing God's will across the stars.

### Organization
- **Imperial Navy Command:** Strategic direction from the throne
- **Heir Family Fleets:** Each great house maintains forces
- **Order of St. Tetrimon:** Religious military order

### Mission Philosophy
The Navy defends the Empire and advances the Reclaiming. Overwhelming force and superior technology crush opposition. God favors the righteous.

### Agent Relationships
Working with Imperial Navy agents demonstrates devotion to the Empire. Standing reflects favor with the divine order.

## Technology

### Laser Superiority
Amarr mastery of energy weapons is unmatched. Lasers require no ammunition - only the endless power of the capacitor. EM and thermal damage burns through shields.

### Armor Doctrine
Amarr ships mount the heaviest armor in New Eden. When God is with you, there is no need to run. Stand and deliver His judgment.

### Capacitor Engineering
Amarr vessels are built around powerful capacitors - energy weapons, active armor repair, energy neutralizers all demand power.

### Ship Design Philosophy
Amarr ships emphasize:
- Energy turrets (pulse and beam lasers)
- Heavy armor tanking
- Capacitor warfare (neutralizers, nosferatu)
- Golden aesthetics reflecting divine glory
- Overwhelming presence over subtlety

## The Five Heir Families

### House Ardishapur
Conservative traditionalists. Strong in military matters. Devout enforcers of religious law.

### House Kador
Cultural patrons. Support the arts and education. Politically moderate.

### House Khanid
Technically a separate kingdom but closely allied. Pragmatic, willing to adopt foreign technology.

### House Sarum
Military powerhouse. Aggressive expansion advocates. Lead forces against the Minmatar.

### House Kor-Azor
Reformed after scandal. Now emphasize justice and proper treatment of slaves. Unusual moderates.

## Relations with Other Empires

### Minmatar Republic - HOSTILE
The escaped slaves and their descendants. The Empire views the Rebellion as theft and heresy. Conflict continues in the warzone and beyond.

### Gallente Federation - COOL
Ideological opposites - democracy versus theocracy. However, direct conflict is rare. The Federation's moralizing about slavery is tiresome but manageable.

### Caldari State - CORDIAL
Both value order and hierarchy. Trade flourishes. Religious differences are politely ignored for mutual profit.

### CONCORD
The Empire participates in CONCORD while asserting sovereign rights over internal matters - including slavery.

## The Slavery Question

### Theological Justification
Amarr doctrine holds that slavery offers pagans a path to redemption through service. Generations of faithful service can earn freedom.

### Current Practice
Millions of slaves, primarily Minmatar but also Ealur, Ni-Kunni, and others. Treatment varies by holder - some are brutal, others relatively benign.

### Reform Movements
Some Amarr advocate for abolition or reform. They are a minority but growing. Empress Jamyl's liberation of some slaves before her death was controversial.

### The Reality
Whatever the theology, slavery is slavery. The Minmatar do not accept theological justifications for chains.

## Pirate Factions in Amarr Space

### Blood Raiders
Primary threat in Amarr low-sec. A heretical cult worshipping blood and practicing ritual exsanguination. They are considered the most vile of pirates by faithful Amarr.

### Sansha's Nation
A cybernetic collective on the Empire's borders. Once Amarr citizens, now twisted by illegal experiments. They raid for captives to convert.

## Historical Events

### The Reclaiming Begins
Amarr first expanded beyond their homeworld with religious fervor, bringing conquered peoples into the faith - often by force.

### The Minmatar Enslavement
The conquest that brought millions of Minmatar into bondage. Remembered as holy victory by Amarr, as the Day of Darkness by Minmatar.

### The Jove Encounter
The Empire's expansion was halted when they encountered the Jove - and were decisively defeated. This humiliation is rarely discussed.

### The Rebellion
When the Minmatar rose up with Gallente and Jove support. The Empire lost millions of slaves and significant territory. The wound still festers.

### Empress Jamyl I
A controversial ruler who returned from apparent death, liberated some slaves, and was eventually killed by a Drifter attack. Her legacy is debated.
